Hi, I am Abinav, nice to meet you!

I am a robotics engineer with a passion for robotics and a strong background in electrical and computer engineering. I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Robotics at Northeastern University. I have developed a wide range of skills essential to the field of robotics. Among these skills, I have developed a deep expertise in computer vision, perception, and motion planning, which are crucial for creating intelligent and autonomous robotic systems. In addition to these core competencies, I have also acquired proficiency in other key areas of robotics. I am adept at developing and implementing sensor fusion techniques to integrate data from multiple sensors, enabling robots to perceive and understand their environment accurately. I have experience in designing and optimizing algorithms for path planning and obstacle avoidance, allowing robots to navigate complex environments with efficiency and safety. Furthermore, I have worked extensively with robotic manipulators, mastering techniques for kinematic modeling, control, and trajectory planning.

Throughout my professional journey, I have gained hands-on experience in developing firmware codes, designing electronic circuits, and implementing sophisticated algorithms for autonomous systems. From creating advanced puck detection algorithms for air hockey robots to optimizing electronic circuits for solenoid mechanisms, I have successfully contributed to various projects. With a drive for innovation and a commitment to excellence, I am eager to continue pushing the boundaries of robotics and leave a lasting impact in the field
